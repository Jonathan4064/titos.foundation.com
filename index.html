About Our Foundation

Our Mission

To empower the community of Bibirioni Ward through sustainable development initiatives in education, healthcare, and sports, fostering a brighter future for all residents.

Our Vision

A thriving Bibirioni Ward where every individual has access to quality education, healthcare, and opportunities for personal and professional growth.

Our History

Founded in 2024 by Titus Kimari, Tito's Foundation began as a small initiative to support underprivileged youth in Bibirioni Ward through sports. Over the years, we've expanded our programs to include education support, healthcare initiatives, and community development projects, becoming a beacon of hope for the entire ward.
